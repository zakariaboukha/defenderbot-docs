# Editorial Policy

## Commitment

DefenderBot is committed to producing helpful, reliable, people-first content about crypto trading bots and automation tools.

Our editorial standard is guided by one core question:

> **Would a reader make a better, more informed decision after reading this page?**

If the answer is no, the page needs more work.

***

## Content Standards

Every published page should:
- Have a clear and specific purpose
- Be based on verified, sourced research
- Add original editorial value beyond what is already available
- Use honest, accurate language without exaggeration
- Include appropriate risk disclosures for financial topics
- Be updated when meaningful changes make it outdated

***

## Transparency Principles

DefenderBot content should be transparent about:
- **Who created it** — authorship or editorial source
- **How it was researched** — what sources and process were used
- **Why it was created** — what question or need the page addresses
- **When it was last updated** — so readers know how current the information is

This matters especially for crypto content where pricing, features, and market conditions change frequently.

***

## Affiliate and Commercial Disclosure

DefenderBot may include affiliate links to platforms reviewed on the site. Where this is the case, it should be disclosed clearly and it should not affect the editorial judgment applied in the review or comparison.

The presence of affiliate links does not mean a product receives a more positive review. Comparisons and rankings are based on research criteria, not commercial relationships.

***

## Corrections Policy

If a factual error is identified in published content, it should be corrected promptly. Significant corrections should be acknowledged in the article.
