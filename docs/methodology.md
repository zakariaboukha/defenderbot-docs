# Methodology

## Overview

DefenderBot uses a structured, multi-source research methodology to produce content that is more accurate, more complete, and more useful than standard affiliate reviews.

This document outlines how research is conducted, how comparisons are structured, and what editorial standards are applied before publication.

***

## Research Process

### Step 1 — Official Source Review
Every review or comparison begins with official product documentation:
- Product website and feature pages
- Official pricing pages
- Exchange compatibility and API documentation
- Release notes and product update announcements
- Terms of service and plan limitations

### Step 2 — Competitive and Industry Research
DefenderBot studies how established publishers, senior review sites, and crypto market leaders cover the same topic. This is done to:
- Understand the broader landscape
- Identify claims that need verification
- Find angles or questions that existing content does not address
- Understand how the topic is being discussed at the highest level in the industry

This research is used as editorial input, not copied output.

### Step 3 — Market Context Analysis
Where relevant, DefenderBot monitors Bitcoin and broader crypto market conditions including:
- Price trends and volatility levels
- Market sentiment (risk-on vs risk-off)
- Exchange news and regulatory developments
- Conditions that affect bot performance or user behavior

This context is added where it genuinely helps the reader make a better decision.

### Step 4 — Structured Evaluation
Using the data gathered, each product is evaluated against a consistent set of criteria. See [comparison-framework.md](comparison-framework.md) for full details.

### Step 5 — Editorial Review and Publishing
Before publication, content is reviewed for:
- Factual accuracy
- Clear structure
- Original editorial value beyond what is available from other sources
- Appropriate risk disclosures

***

## Content Update Policy

Content should be reviewed and updated when:
- Pricing changes
- Exchange compatibility changes
- New features are released or removed
- Market conditions shift in a way that affects the context of a review

***

## Disclaimer

DefenderBot content is for informational purposes only. Readers should verify all product, pricing, and feature details directly with official providers before making decisions.
