# Market Coverage Policy

## Overview

DefenderBot includes Bitcoin and crypto market context in content where it improves the reader's understanding of trading bot selection, performance, and risks.

***

## Why Market Context Matters

Crypto trading bots do not exist in isolation. The conditions under which a user deploys a bot significantly affect:
- Which strategy types are appropriate
- What risk levels are reasonable
- Whether automated trading is likely to perform well or poorly
- How user expectations should be calibrated

Ignoring market conditions in bot reviews would leave readers with an incomplete picture.

***

## What Market Coverage Includes

DefenderBot may track and comment on:
- Bitcoin price direction and key trend context
- Crypto market volatility levels
- Broader market sentiment (risk-on vs risk-off)
- Exchange-related developments
- Regulatory news that affects bot platforms or exchanges
- Market cycles relevant to automation strategy selection

***

## Editorial Boundaries

Market coverage is included to provide context for readers making product decisions.

DefenderBot does not:
- Publish price predictions
- Offer financial advice
- Promise returns from any strategy or bot
- Recommend trading or investment actions

All market commentary is clearly labeled as educational context and not financial guidance.
