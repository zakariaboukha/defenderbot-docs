## About Crypto Trading Bots

**What is a crypto trading bot?**
A crypto trading bot is software that automatically executes trades on a cryptocurrency exchange according to predefined rules or strategies.

**Are crypto trading bots safe to use?**
Crypto trading bots vary significantly in quality, security, and transparency. Users should always verify how API keys are handled, whether funds remain in their own exchange account, and what security practices the platform follows.

**Is DefenderBot financial advice?**
No. All content on DefenderBot is for informational and educational purposes only. Users should do their own research and consult appropriate professionals before making any investment or trading decisions.
