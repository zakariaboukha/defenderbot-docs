## What Is DefenderBot?

DefenderBot is an independent crypto trading bot research and comparison platform. We publish in-depth reviews, structured comparisons, and editorial analysis to help traders — from beginners to experienced users — evaluate automation tools with clarity and confidence.

We don't just repeat marketing claims. We research platforms using official documentation, pricing pages, exchange compatibility data, and real competitive analysis. We also monitor the broader Bitcoin and crypto market to give readers context that actually affects how bots perform and which tools make sense at any given time.

***

## Why DefenderBot Exists

The crypto trading bot market is crowded with platforms making bold promises. Most comparison content online is either:
- Written by affiliates repeating the same bullet points
- Outdated within months as features change
- Missing real market context

DefenderBot was built to fix that by producing structured, honest, research-backed content about trading bots and automation platforms.

***

## What We Cover

- **Bot Reviews** — Independent reviews of crypto trading bots with detailed feature analysis
- **Bot Comparisons** — Side-by-side comparisons using consistent evaluation criteria
- **Exchange Compatibility** — Which bots work with which exchanges and how well
- **Pricing Analysis** — Transparent breakdown of pricing, plans, and limitations
- **Market Context** — Bitcoin and crypto market conditions relevant to bot selection and performance
- **Competitor Intelligence** — How the best review sites and crypto publishers are covering the space

***

## How Our Content Is Created

Our research process draws from multiple layers:

1. **Official product sources** — websites, docs, pricing pages, feature announcements
2. **Exchange documentation** — API compatibility, trading limits, integration details
3. **Market monitoring** — Bitcoin trends, crypto market sentiment, volatility, risk environment
4. **Competitor and industry research** — how senior publishers, established review sites, and market leaders cover the same topics
5. **Structured comparison methodology** — original editorial synthesis, not rewritten copies

Full details in [docs/methodology.md](docs/methodology.md)
